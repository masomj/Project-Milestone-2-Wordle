Wordle

How to play?

Guess the WORDLE in five tries.

Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit

After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

If a letter is green, it is in the word and in the correct spot.

If a letter is orange, it is in the word but in the incorrect spot.

If a letter is grey, it is not in the word in any spot.
